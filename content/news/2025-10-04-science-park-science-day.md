Science Park Science Day took place during the [Weekend of Science](https://weekendvandewetenschap.nl/). On this day, the Faculty of Science (FNWI) and its research institutes joined forces with five organizations to introduce interested visitors to all kinds of science.

The Science Day at Amsterdam Science Park is for everyone between the ages of 6 and 106 who is interested in science and wants to learn more about it. The whIRLwind team gave demonstrations throughout the day, during which visitors could watch robots playing football matches.
