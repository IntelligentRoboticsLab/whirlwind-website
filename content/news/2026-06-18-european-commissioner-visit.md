On Thursday 11 June, Ekaterina Zaharieva, the European Commissioner for Startups, Research and Innovation, stopped by the Faculty of Science of the University of Amsterdam at Science park, during a visit focused on current developments in quantum technology and artificial intelligence.

Minister Rianne Letschert (Education, Culture and Science) and Minister Heleen Herbert (Economic Affairs and Climate Policy) joined the delegation, which was welcomed by rector magnificus Peter-Paul Verbeek, dean of science Susan te Pas and university professor Robbert Dijkgraaf.

As part of the visit team whIRLwind provided a short demonstration of our robots playing a football match. It was a great chance to show the Commissioner and the ministers what the team has been working on the past few months.
