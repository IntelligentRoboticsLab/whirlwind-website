On 16 April, the Princess of Orange visited Amsterdam Science Park, including a demo by the whIRLwind team in the Intelligent Robotics Lab.

Princess Amalia was in Amsterdam for an introductory visit at the invitation of Mayor Femke Halsema. The theme was Amsterdam Tech City, with a special focus on AI and sustainable solar energy.

In LAB42, the Princess and the Mayor met the robots of the Intelligent Robotics Lab. After a brief introduction, the robots played a fierce 3x3 football match. The Princess then took part in a roundtable discussion and visited the AMOLF labs.
